#include <boost/simd/config.hpp>

Fast math behavior option.

By defining this symbol or passing -ffast-math option to the compiler, the behavior of Boost.SIMD mathematical functions will be in accordance with standard definition of fast IEEE math which includes:

  • No handling of NaN values
  • No handling of infinite values
  • No handling of denormals
  • No handling of -0.
